Research Facilitator Program

This program provides funding to NIT Warangal UG and PG students whose work is chosen for presentation in top national/ international conferences and internships

Selection Criteria

NIT Warangal UG and PG students are eligible. Students must receive selection confirmation from conference organizers or internship providers.

Selection Process

  • Student mails selection confirmation and conference/internship details
  • Lakshya committee evaluates conference/internship ranking
  • Funding is provided based on conference/internship ranking and after student confirms participation

Funding Criteria

Funding amount is based on the releative difficulty of obtaining internship/slot in conference and the quality of the hosting organization
