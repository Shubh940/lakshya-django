Innovation Support Program

The Innovation Support Program is a step towards building an atmosphere of research, innovation and entrepreneurship at NIT Warangal.

The Innovation Support Program involves:

  • Funding and guiding innovative ideas from students and faculty
  • Protecting IPR resulting from such ideas
  • Encourage students to take up technology entrepreneurship
